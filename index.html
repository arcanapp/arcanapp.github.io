About Dr. Rudra Jani

Dr. Rudra Jani is a Rehabilitation Psychologist holding a PGDRP from BM Institute, Ahmedabad. Dr. Jani specializes in providing psychological assessment and therapy for children with special needs, cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), and career counseling. Counseling services are also available for the adult population.

Our Services

Child Counseling

Specialized counseling services for children with various psychological and emotional needs.

Adult Counseling

Professional counseling services tailored to address the needs of adults facing personal or professional challenges.

Career Counseling

Expert guidance and support to help individuals make informed decisions about their careers.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Evidence-based therapeutic approach to address a variety of psychological issues.
